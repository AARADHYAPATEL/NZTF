üí° Why Does It Matter?

Greenhouse gases cause global warming and climate change by trapping heat in the atmosphere. Sea levels rise, wildfires increase, extreme weather events occur, and biodiversity declines as a result. This process accelerates as we emit more.

The good news is that minor adjustments can have a significant impact. You can actively combat climate change by monitoring and lowering your carbon footprint.
